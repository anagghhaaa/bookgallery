AVALUDE KADHA

P PATHMARAJAN

"Avalude Kadha" is a Malayalam autobiography written by Padhmarajan, a renowned Indian author. The title translates to "Her Story" in English. Published in 1988, the book is a poignant narrative that delves into the author's personal experiences, particularly focusing on his relationship with his mother.

RAM C/O ANANDHI

AKHIL P DHARMAJAN

"Ram C/O Anandhi" is a novel written by Akhil P. Dharmajan, an emerging author in contemporary Indian literature. Published in 2019, this novel explores the themes of love, relationships, and societal expectations in the modern context.

BALYAKALASAGHI

VAIKOM MUHAMMED BASHEER

"Balyakala Sakhi" is a Malayalam novel written by Vaikom Muhammad Basheer, one of the most celebrated writers in Malayalam literature. It was first published in 1944. The title translates to "Childhood Friend" in English.
